# CCAO-F Trainer Course

A complete training course for the **Claude Certified Associate – Foundations (CCAO-F)** exam, built for trainers preparing cohorts of candidates.

## Sources and integrity

All exam facts (format, blueprint, weights, scoring, policies) come from the **official CCAO-F Exam Guide v1.0 (July 2026)**, available from the Anthropic Partner Academy — download and read it in full; it is the authoritative reference. Feature and product content is grounded in official Anthropic documentation (docs.claude.com, support.claude.com, anthropic.com); each module cites its sources. All practice questions are **original items written in the exam's style** — real exam items are NDA-protected and are never reproduced here.

## How each module is structured

1. **Learner content** — concepts and applied guidance for every blueprint objective in the domain
2. **Trainer notes** — teaching tips, discussion prompts, common misconceptions, suggested hands-on exercises
3. **Practice questions** — original multiple-choice items with answer rationales
4. **Sources** — links to the official documentation used

## Suggested use

Master the modules yourself first (Domains 2 and 4 carry the most weight — 37% combined), run the delivery plan in the [Exam Overview](00-exam-overview-and-study-plan.md) with your cohort, and use the mock exam as a timed final readiness check (rule of thumb: roughly 75–80%+ raw, with no domain far behind — the real exam's 720/1000 cut is a scaled score, not a raw percentage).
